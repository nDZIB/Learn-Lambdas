A cooking api.
to cook a meal, you need  to
get the ingredients and then follow the cooking procedure.

the api enables one to add a recipe or retrieve the recipe for a given meal:

add a recipe: Recipe.cook(meal).get(incredients).alsoGet(ingredients)....do(procedureOne).next(nextProcedure)...(done);

to get a recipe: Recipe.retrieveProcedure(meal).retrieve()
